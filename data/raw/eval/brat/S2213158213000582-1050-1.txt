•Auditory encoding in schizophrenia (SZ) and healthy controls (HC) was examined.•Distributed source localization provided whole-brain measures from 30 to 130 ms.•Abnormalities were observed in superior temporal gyrus (STG) auditory areas in SZ.•Encoding abnormalities were also observed in frontal and supramarginal gyrus areas.•SZ shows abnormalities in multiple nodes of a distributed auditory network.
